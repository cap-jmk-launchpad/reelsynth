## Fixes applied this pass

1. **Panel + WT strip corner radius:** 8px → 10px (`RADIUS_SM`) to match `--radius-sm`.
2. **Header:** wordmark-only shell (removed Open/Save/MIDI/CPU row not in S1 mockup).
3. **Header/footer vertical center:** `set_min_height(rect.height())` for 48px / 36px alignment.
4. **Hero padding:** `SPACE_MD` (20px) inset matching `.rs-spectrum-hero`.
5. **Footer piano toggle:** custom `.rs-toggle` sizing (4×10 pad, 6px radius) vs selectable label.
6. **Footer status dot:** 6px green circle vs text bullet.
7. **Rail width calc:** `ui.available_width()` inside frame margin (was outer-rect double count risk).
8. **Disabled panel title:** `AMP ENVELOPE (locked)` casing matches mockup `::after`.
9. **Black keys:** explicit `right: -29%` slot math from mockups.css.
